About me

My name is Ilyas Benhssine, 20 year old, born in Morocco Rabat, I’m a Full Stack developer & software engineering student in the beginning of my career, i’m also a software analyste in the automotive industry

Background

I grew up in a moroccan blue-collar household, ever since i was a little kid, technologie & automobile were my passion. I still remember sitting in front of my playstation playing gran turismo on the Nordschleif with the Mercedes Benz Evo 2. As i went throught the races it was almost magic, being able to get a piece of plastic, some wires & electricity to lift you from your boring room to the nordschleif and drive the car of your dreams. Under the constant pression of school and house to do stuff correctly & the right way i developped perfectionism which helped me to push the limits & do the best i can, and also depriving me from feeling the satisfaction from doing stuff as i fell like i can always do better & i should do better.

Technologie

At the age of 6 I had my own personal computer, since I was fascinated by the concept of having a machine where you can express yourself however you want, create whatever you want, limited only by my own imagination and ingenuity. I managed to create my first html websites. This passion carried me throughout my education.

Now in the industry, that passion stays with me. There is certainly more to learn, more problems to solve, and more to create and build. And for that I am very grateful.

Automobile

Throughout elementary school, my father worked with the government, he had luxury work cars, he always waited for me in front of the school leaning on the car. I have always admired this metal machine with an intricately designed motor and 4 wheels that can take you anywhere you want quickly while comfortably seated in a leather seat.
